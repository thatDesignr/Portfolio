Startweek

Agriculture of the future

For the startweek, the theme was "Future". We got asked to think of how the future of the agriculture would look in the year 2050. We got 3 days the time to think of an idea and to make a prototype; a veritcal farm with 3 layers: underwater, air, and underground.

Pictures of the prototype

We made the prototype out of cardboard and Play-doh.

UI for to manage your farms

Besides a physical prototype, we also made a digital prototype that shows how you could manage your farms all over the world.
