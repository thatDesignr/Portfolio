Project: Branding

Veneman en de Groot

Woody Veneman en Truus de Groot are two artists that make various types of music. Together, they invented the genre "Neo-Smartlappen", which is derived from the original genre "Smartlappen". Their intentions are to make a Smartlappen genre that also is for the younger generation. To support this, we got asked to make a new branding for Veneman en de Groot. The goal of this branding project was to target a younger target audience, sell LPs, and most importantly keep dive bars alive by inviting people to join the upcoming Veneman en de Groot pubcrawl through Eindhoven.

Moodboard

We started off with a moodboard to get the general style set in stone.

Stylescape

Once we had a general style set with a moodboard, we finetuned the style in a stylescape. This style scape includes all graphical elements of the whole brand.

The style of the branding consists out of typical Dutch elements like tulleeps and rain. It was important to the client to depict the sadness in neo-smartlappen, we achieved that by adding rain, broken hearts and using a dark style in general.

Logo Design

The logo we made is a logo type consisting out of bright neon colors. The bold letters are easy to read to give it a modern look. The pink/purple colors represents passion and intensity, two emotions often assosicated with the smartlappen genre.".

Brand guide

To have unity and rules in the brand we created, we made a brand guide for future designers to use. This brand guide consists out of the misson of Veneman en de Groot, a logo guide, color guide, typography information, assets, and media expression examples.

Media Expressions

We made a content strategy for Veneman en de Groot which includes social media, posters, stickers and merchendise. This is a great way to promote your brand and connect with your fans.

Presentation

At the end of the project we presented our work to the client.
